Learning outcomes
After completing the course the student is expected to have clinical expertise in/to:
 

Be able to use her physiotherapy knowledge in multi-professional collaboration
Able to work independently in the tasks fysioterapeutens work in the specific professional field requires
Be able to search and compile evidence-based information based on a specific topic within a subject area in physiotherapy
Students compile their own learning goals of enhanced field.
