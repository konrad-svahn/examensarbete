Cor-house, the centre of student union activities, is located a few hundred meters from Arcada main building. Cor-house was opened in 2007 and was built exclusively for students. Today the house serves as an office for Arcada student union, ASK, as well as for most of the student associations (more information about the student union and the student associations can be found at www.asken.fi External link

. In addition to offices, you will also find working spaces and a sauna at the Cor-house. Meeting rooms and the sauna can be rented out for various purposes. Events are organized by both ASK and the student associations.
