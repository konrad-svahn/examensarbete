Incoming Exchange Students



 









We would like to welcome students from our partner institutions to do an exchange at Arcada!

Arcada has about 90 partner universities around the world and welcomes about 70 exchange students per year. Around fifty nationalities are represented at Arcada, partly within the English degree programmes and partly through student, teacher and expert exchange.
Information about coming on exchange to Arcada: arcada.fi/en/study-arcada/exchange-studies-arcada External link
