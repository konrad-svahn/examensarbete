Registering your Arcada IT-Account

Your IT-account consists of username & password, file storage, email addresses and user information.
In order to activate your account, you can visit our Accounts Portal at apps.arcada.fi/aas/en External link. The same link is used to reset your password in case you want to change it.
More information about Arcada User Account can be found here:
https://start.arcada.fi/en/user-account-and-password
