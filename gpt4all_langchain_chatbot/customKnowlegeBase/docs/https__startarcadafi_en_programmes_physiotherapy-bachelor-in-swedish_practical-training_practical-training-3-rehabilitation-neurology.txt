Practical Training 3 - Rehabilitation, Neurology



 









Learning Outcomes

After the practial training he student can:

assess, plan, do and evaluate rehabilitation and physiotherapy for clients with neurological diseases or injuries.
can as an expert work in team, and together with clients, relatives, other professionals and rehabilitation organisations can evaluate the effect of physiotherapy.
can supervise and discuss with people in different ages and from other context
can critically look upon own practical skills and is willing to develop oneself and physiotherapy overall
have knowledge of the ethical principles and follow them in physiotherapy practice
