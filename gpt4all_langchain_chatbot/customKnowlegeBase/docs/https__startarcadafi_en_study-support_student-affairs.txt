Student Affairs help students with a wide variety of questions and processes

Services for Students
Arcada's study coordinators are happy to help you with the following:

Degree certificates

Applying for your degree certificate
Certified copies of degree certificates


Certificates for students

Transcript of records and certificate of study
Other individual certificates


Student discounts

KELA meal subsidy card
HSL form for local transport and information about discounts for long distance transport
Information about student cards


Applying for accreditation or validation of courses taken at other universities
Registration for the academic year as present/absent
Applying for an extension of your right to study
Changes in the student registry

Contact information
Name change
Personal identity number



Note that all degree programmes also have a Study Counselor, who is focused on that specific degree programme's students' curricula and courses. If you need help planning your studies contact your Study Counselor - you can find their contact information on this webpage.
Not sure who to talk to? Call us or come talk to us during our office hours (see below) and we'll help you or find someone who can help you.
