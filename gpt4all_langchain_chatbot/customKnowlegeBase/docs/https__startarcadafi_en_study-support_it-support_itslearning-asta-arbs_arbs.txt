ARBS is used for finding course time tables, teacher time tables, available computer labs etc.
Course time tables on your phone
You can save your time table from ARBS to your phone, tablet or computer. Download the iCal-file from ARBS.
Choose first View my schedule, click iCal.
Your calendar should automatically ask you if you want to save the ARBS-information in the calendar.
Please note, this schedule information will not be updated after you have saved it on your device. If a lecture is cancelled, you will not see this reflected in your calendar.
