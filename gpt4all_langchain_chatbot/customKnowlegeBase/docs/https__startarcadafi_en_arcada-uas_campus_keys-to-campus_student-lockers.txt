Student lockers



 









Booking student lockers

The student lockers (found on the first floor near the main entrance) are booked electronically for one academic year at a time and the lockers are emptied before the summer. You can lock the lockers with your own padlock.
How do I book a locker?
To book a locker, click here External link. The form will automatically notify which lockers are available. You can only book one locker. The lockers 1177-1266 are high lockers.
