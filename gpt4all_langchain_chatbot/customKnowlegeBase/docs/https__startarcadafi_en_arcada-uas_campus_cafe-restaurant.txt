Cafe & Restaurant



 









Restaurant Compass Group can be found between the small and big square in the Arcada main building. The restaurant serves a variety of lunch choices, one of which is always vegetarian from Monday to Friday.

In addition to serving lunch, Amica also serves breakfast. From the cafe you can buy home-baked pastries, salty snacks and refreshments. More information about opening hours can be found here. External link
The restaurant is covered by KELA meal subsidy, meaning that the student is granted a discount on presentation of a student card with a valid term or academic year decal or with Kela meal subsidy card. Always remember to show your student card to get lunch at a discounted price.
