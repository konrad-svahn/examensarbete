Application fees

Students applying for extended right to study, re-enrollment or renewed right to study in accordance with the Rector´s decision (20/2010), must pay a fee of 50 euros to Arcadas bank account in Nordea FI 5315 9630 0009 7776 (Swift: NDEAFIHH). The fee regulation is derived from Decree 1440/2014, 3 §.
The name of the student should be written in the message field when making the payment, and the receipt should be attached to the online application form. Applications without a payment receipt will not be considered.
