Advanced Practical Training (Social Services)



 









Learning Outcomes
At the end of the course the student is
expected to be able to
 

 apply theoretical knowledge in working life
develop his/her skills in professional interaction with service users in different socialpedagogical contexts
develops his/her professional role and identity and reflects critically on ethical and other values
The aim of the field placement assignment is to activate student in research, development and/or innovation activities.
