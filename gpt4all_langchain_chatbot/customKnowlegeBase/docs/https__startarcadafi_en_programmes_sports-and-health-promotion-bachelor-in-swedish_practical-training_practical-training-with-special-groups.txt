Practical Training with Special Groups



 









Learning Outcomes




At the end of the course the student is expected to be able to

control and perform in a professional way in all tasks which belong to the activities of different target groups.
The student should be able to control the whole work process and also its different parts.
The student can set relevant goals and adapt different forms of physical activity that support the individual's health.
