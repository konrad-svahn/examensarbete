When you leave Arcada



 









Things to keep in mind regarding your IT-account and E-mail

When you finish your studies or your employment on Arcada, the IT-account (incl. email address) will be closed in 7 days. Therefore it is good to:

Go through all your email
Save a local copy of your Arcada email (if needed)
Save your email contacts (if needed)
Change your email information on various internet services
Inform your colleeges about your new contact information
Save files from the H-drive
Save files from OneDrive
Save material from itslearning
etc.
