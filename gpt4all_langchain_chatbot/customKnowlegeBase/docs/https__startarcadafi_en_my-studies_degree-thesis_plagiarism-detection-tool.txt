Plagiarism detection tool - Ouriginal (Urkund)



 









At Arcada we use Ouriginal (former Urkund) as our Plagiarism detection tool. 

There are three options for plagiarism checking documents via Ouriginal:

Ouriginal is used in the assignment on itslearning
The student sends the document as an e-mail to the teacher's analysis address (the teacher finds it when he has logged in to the record) - Read more here External link
The teacher logs in to Urkund External link (via Shibboleth Login - Select Arcada) and loads the documents there.
Please also familiarize your self with our guidelines for using AI.
