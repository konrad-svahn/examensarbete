Instructions for Medication Passport (Lääkehoitopassi)



 








Go to the page https://www.laakehoitopassi.fi/ External link and choose the language you prefer.
Look at the Introduction video for students.
Register to the Passport. You will need your student number. You can only use your Arcada-mail. State your group name as NS16 without any spaces and with NS first.
Please register as soon as possible. We will look at the Passport together at a lecture.
